📖 How to play

Rules

• Left drag draw, right drag pan, scroll zoom
• Enter/R to send cells, ESC to cancel

• Base: free placement. Outside: costs energy
• Conway: 2-3 neighbors survive, 3 = birth
• Enemy cells in your base drain 1 HP each
• Base destroyed at 0 HP, HP regens slowly
• Dead cells earn $1 — spend on upgrades
• Upgrades: base size, HP, energy, regen rate
• Outposts: $100K, 10×10, needs explored area
• Click own cells to delete instantly
